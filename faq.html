Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I donate through your website?

You can donate easily by clicking the "Donate" button and following the instructions. We accept a variety of payment methods for your convenience.

2. Where does my donation go?

Your donation directly supports our ongoing projects, helping those in need. You can read more about how funds are allocated on our donation breakdown page.

3. Is there a minimum amount required to donate?

No, you can donate any amount that fits your budget. Every contribution, big or small, makes a difference.

4. Can I set up a recurring donation?

Yes! You have the option to make your donation recurring on a monthly, quarterly, or yearly basis.

5. Is my donation secure?

Absolutely. We use a secure, encrypted payment system to ensure your financial information is safe and protected.

6. Will I receive a donation receipt?

Yes, you will receive an email confirmation and a receipt immediately after your donation is processed.

7. Can I donate in someone else’s name?

Yes, we offer an option to donate on behalf of someone else. You can include a message for them, and we’ll notify them of your gift.

8. Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to our organization are tax-deductible. We will provide the necessary receipt for your tax records.

9. Can I choose which cause my donation supports?

Yes, when you donate, you’ll have the option to select a specific cause or project you want to support, or you can allow us to use the funds where they are most needed.

10. What if I have a question about my donation?

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your donation, feel free to contact our support team. We're here to help!
